Compare the results of this test to the basic pingpong test. On shared networks, you are likely to see performance that is very roughly 1/(size/2) of the performance of the basic pingpong test. Multistage networks will provide performance that degrades more slowly as the number of processors increases; there may be steps indicating major changes in interconnection (e.g., a system made up of 4 x 4 crossbars may show changes at 4, 16, 64, etc. processors).
